Recent Projects

Blossom Cafe

花样年华

Blossom Cafe is where romance lingers over Asian delicacies, a neighborhood gem inspired by the timeless 'In the Mood for Love'. The name evokes the bloom of youth and love, yet at Blossom, Echoing the nuanced tale of Mr. Chow and Mrs. Su, we invite you to a world where love unfolds in its most exquisite forms—beyond time, age, and the ephemeral. Here, every sip and bite is a chance to savor life's unfolding beauty. Welcome to Blossom, where every moment is an invitation to love.

Feminine Space

"SHE"

This magazine beautifully intertwines two stories of love and longing: an unknown woman's lifelong infatuation from Stefan Zweig's 'Letter from an Unknown Woman', and the complex romance of a young French girl and a wealthy Chinese man from Marguerite Duras' 'Lover'. It features poignant excerpts from both, highlighting the depths and complexities of unrequited and forbidden love.

Peony Dreams: On the other side of sleep

National Dance Project

The National Dance Project (NDP) Peony Dreams - On the Other Side of Sleep which is awarded the grant from the New England Foundation for the Arts (NEFA). I designed brochure and posters for the performance which is premiered at La Mama theater in New York (2019) and in Ringling Museum (2020) for campaign's advertising. And a series of postcards for NYC/MDF (Multidisciplinary Festival in Long Island) exhibition.

The KuBe Art Center

logo

The KuBe Art Center encompasses the Beacon location of Ethan Cohen Gallery, experimental art spaces, 48 artists studios, and commercial office spaces. Acting as a platform for artists to continually create and present work to collectors, curators, and fellow artists. During my internship there, I was assigned to design the Logo for KuBe Art Center and a poster series for the exhibition of contemporary portraiture - Artist as Subject.

Deck of Playing Cards

Packaging Design

This playing card deck brings the Tang Dynasty's splendor to life, with hearts representing key figures: Emperor Xuanzong as power, consort Yang Yuhuan as beauty, Gao Lishi as loyalty, poet Li Bai as romance, and An Lushan as betrayal, each card weaving a piece of this golden era's rich tapestry.
